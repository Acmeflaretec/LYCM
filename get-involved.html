Get Involved with LYCM

Join LYCM and Make a Difference in Part-Time or Full-Time Voluntary Positions!

Are you passionate about transforming the lives of underprivileged children? Love You Children Mission (LYCM) invites dedicated individuals like you to become a part of our impactful team. We offer various part-time and full-time voluntary positions where you can contribute to the well-being and education of these children:

Your commitment in these part-time or full-time voluntary positions can make a significant difference in the lives of these children. Join us at LYCM and be a part of creating a brighter future. Together, we can empower these young minds and build a stronger, more compassionate society.

Apply now and be the change you want to see!

Social Worker

Make a direct impact on the community by addressing social issues and working towards positive change, fostering a healthier environment for growth.

Volunteer Teacher

Inspire young minds by sharing your knowledge and expertise. Help bridge the education gap in slum areas and ignite a passion for learning.

Psychologist

Provide crucial support to children facing psychological challenges, offering guidance and assistance to help them overcome obstacles and thrive

Mentor

Become a guiding light for these children, helping them navigate challenges and discover their potential, instilling confidence and hope for the future.

Awareness Advocate

Champion the rights of underprivileged children and raise awareness about the importance of education and social support, mobilizing the community for collective action.
