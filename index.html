How We Started

Love You Children Mission (LYCM) was founded by Mr. Linos David in 2010, inspired by his experience with young drug addicts. With support from Dr. Abraham, Mr. David initiated efforts to rehabilitate youth, leading to the creation of LYCM. We began our work in the Goyala Dairy slum, focusing on education and care for children from a young age.

Love

At LYCM, we believe in the power of love to transform lives. Our programs are designed to provide not just education, but also emotional and psychological support to children in need.

Compassion

We approach every child with compassion, addressing their unique needs and challenges. Our team works tirelessly to ensure each child receives the care and attention they deserve.

Charity

Charity is at the heart of our mission. By supporting LYCM, you contribute to providing education, healthcare, and shelter, helping to build a better future for underprivileged children.

Our Programs. Support Us

At Love Your Children Mission (LYCM), we focus on providing education, healthcare, and shelter to underprivileged children. Your support helps us make a meaningful difference in their lives.

Educational Support

We provide tuition, school fees, and educational resources to poor children and orphans, ensuring they have the opportunity to learn and grow.

Nursing Sponsorship

LYCM sponsors nursing education for deserving students who have passed their 12th grade, empowering them with the skills needed for a successful career in healthcare.

Hostel Facilities

We provide safe and comfortable hostel accommodations for poor children and orphans, giving them a secure place to live while they pursue their education.

Get Involved with LYCM

Become a Volunteer

Are you passionate about transforming the lives of underprivileged children? Love You Children Mission (LYCM) invites dedicated individuals to join our impactful team. We offer various part-time and full-time voluntary positions where you can contribute to the well-being and education of these children.

Happy Giving

Your support helps us provide education, healthcare, and shelter to children in need. By contributing to LYCM, you become a part of their journey towards a brighter future. Every donation makes a difference and brings joy to those who need it most.

Donation

If you would like to donate to this noble cause, please contact Love You Children Mission. Your contributions support our programs in education, nursing sponsorship, and hostel facilities for underprivileged children.

Social Worker

Make a direct impact on the community by addressing social issues and working towards positive change. Your efforts will foster a healthier environment for growth and development for these children.

Volunteer Teacher

Inspire young minds by sharing your knowledge and expertise. Help bridge the education gap in slum areas and ignite a passion for learning. Your involvement will play a crucial role in the educational development of these children.

Psychologist

Provide crucial support to children facing psychological challenges, offering guidance and assistance to help them overcome obstacles and thrive. Your expertise will be essential in nurturing their mental and emotional well-being.

Mentor

Become a guiding light for these children, helping them navigate challenges and discover their potential. Your mentorship will instill confidence and hope for their future, empowering them to achieve their goals.

Awareness Advocate

Champion the rights of underprivileged children and raise awareness about the importance of education and social support. Mobilize the community for collective action to support and uplift these children.
