Our Programs

At Love You Children Mission (LYCM), we focus on providing education, healthcare, and shelter to underprivileged children. Your support helps us make a meaningful difference in their lives.

Educational Support

We provide tuition, school fees, and educational resources to poor children and orphans, ensuring they have the opportunity to learn and grow.

Nursing Sponsorship

LYCM sponsors nursing education for deserving students who have passed their 12th grade, empowering them with the skills needed for a successful career in healthcare.

Hostel Facilities

We provide safe and comfortable hostel accommodations for poor children and orphans, giving them a secure place to live while they pursue their education.

Love

At LYCM, we believe in the power of love to transform lives. Our programs are designed to provide not just education, but also emotional and psychological support to children in need.

Compassion

We approach every child with compassion, addressing their unique needs and challenges. Our team works tirelessly to ensure each child receives the care and attention they deserve.
